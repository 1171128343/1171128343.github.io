Flash Animation

Spring, 2016

This is the first time I played with Adobe Flash. This assignment is assigned to the class by my Graphic Design teacher. The goal for this assignment is to create an animated figure to run across the forest from day to night. When I first started the project, I was very exciting because i'm very into animation. Even Though the project is based on the bone tool, but working with frames and timing caught on my attention. When I look back at this video, I notices that the character move faster at the second layer and even faster at the far back. It is caused by the length of the clip. All three walking clips are the same length, but the character is smaller and the scene is farer, which makes it looks like the character is speeding up. Next time dealing with timing, I will not make the same mistake again.
